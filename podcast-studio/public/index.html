What's included

Everything You Need

// built for serious podcasters, not just calls

🎙️
Individual Track Recording
Each participant's audio+video is recorded separately as a local .webm file. No quality loss from compression.
🎬
Combined Session Recording
A composed canvas recording captures the full session layout — grid, spotlight, or picture-in-picture.
🌐
True Browser-Based WebRTC
Low-latency peer-to-peer streaming via WebRTC. No plugin, no app install. Works in Chrome, Firefox, Edge.
🎛️
Host Director Controls
Manage layouts, admit/kick guests, control recording, mute participants, and monitor everyone's stream.
🔗
Shareable Invite Link
Share a simple link with guests. They join via browser, no login required. Waiting room approval flow included.
📐
Flexible Layouts
Switch between Grid, Spotlight, Picture-in-Picture, and Side-by-Side layouts on the fly during recording.
💬
Live Session Chat
Built-in text chat for backchannel communication without interrupting the podcast flow.
📁
Recording Library
All recordings saved to server with SQLite metadata. Download individual tracks or combined output anytime.
🟢
Local Green Room
Test your mic, camera, and lighting before going live with dual-camera preview and audio monitoring.
Workflow

How it Works

1

Host Creates Session

Enter your name, session title and get a unique session ID + shareable invite link

2

Guests Join via Link

Up to 3 guests click the invite link, enter their name, and wait in the virtual lobby

3

Host Admits & Records

Host admits guests from the waiting room, sets layout, then starts recording with one click

4

Download Individual Tracks

After session, all individual recordings and combined output are saved and available to download
